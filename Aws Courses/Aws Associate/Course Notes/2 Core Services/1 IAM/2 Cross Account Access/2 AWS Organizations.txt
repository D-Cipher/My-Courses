AWS Organizations

AWS Organizations -> Offers policy based management for multiple AWS accounts
- Provides policy framework for multiple AWS accounts
- Create groups of accounts then apply policies to all accounts
- Eventually consistent

Consolidated Billing -> enables you to set up a single payment method for all AWS accounts in your organization and view combined charges.

Service Control Policy -> Policy that specifies the services and actions users in the member account has access to.

Organization -> collection of AWS accounts organized and managed centrally

Master Account -> Account to manage your organization, cannot be changed

Member Accounts -> Child accounts that are managed by master account

Administrative root -> Top level container in your organization hierarchy

Organization Unit (OU) -> Group of AWS accounts in an organization
